One signal. One second. One life saved.

Engineering autonomous safety for those who protect our nation — built in India, for India.

Our Mission

To eliminate preventable deaths in aerial operations through intelligent, autonomous safety systems — engineered for the realities of Indian defence and aviation.

We build technology that responds when human reaction cannot. Every line of code, every sensor calibration, every test flight is driven by a single purpose: bring every paratrooper and aviator home.

Our Vision

An India where no aerial fatality is caused by a failure that technology could have prevented.

We envision ParaDigm AAD as the standard safety layer across Indian defence training, NCC programs, and commercial skydiving — a Made-in-India solution trusted as absolutely as the parachute itself.

Our Journey

From a Classroom in Bhilwara
to the Skies Above India

Kshitiej Tactical Technology Pvt. Ltd. was founded by five engineering students from Rajasthan Technical University, Bhilwara — a small city with an outsized ambition. What began as a final-year project responding to a real defence gap became a registered company with a working prototype, active aerial trials, and a clear path to deployment.

We are not a large corporation. We are five engineers who chose to build something that matters — an indigenous AAD system that can match international counterparts at a fraction of the cost, and be accessible to every Indian defence unit that needs it.
