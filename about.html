Our Mission & Vision

At Kshitej Defence, we develop cutting-edge defense technologies that safeguard our nation and empower innovation in aerospace, surveillance, and safety systems. Our vision is a world secured by autonomous precision, where ethical technology defends peace.

Our Journey: From Concept to Command

Kshitej Defence and Aerospace Tech is a pioneering startup emerging from the small yet ambitious district of Bhilwara, Rajasthan — founded by passionate batchmates of Rajasthan Technical University. What started as a student vision soon evolved into a mission to design, test, and deploy indigenous aerospace systems capable of redefining India’s defense frontier.
