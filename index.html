ParaDigm

ParaDigm The Automatic Activation Device (AAD) is a cutting-edge safety system designed to save lives in critical situations. Our technology automatically deploys parachutes when detecting freefall beyond safe altitude thresholds.

Built with aerospace-grade sensors and redundant fail-safe mechanisms, the AAD continuously monitors altitude, descent rate, and atmospheric pressure in real-time. It makes split-second decisions with 99.9% accuracy, ensuring deployment even when the operator is incapacitated.

Trusted by defence forces, skydiving professionals, and aerospace programs worldwide, our AAD has successfully deployed over 50,000 times, saving countless lives. Each unit undergoes rigorous testing to meet international safety standards.
